What gets deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed within 30 days:

  • Your account (email address and credentials)
  • All income and expense transaction records
  • All budget goals and spending limits
  • All household and settings data

Data stored locally on your device will be cleared the next time you uninstall the app.

Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your financial data will be lost.

How to request deletion

Send an email to our support address with the subject line "Delete My Account". Include the email address associated with your BudgetBox account.

  1. Tap the button below to open an email draft
  2. Make sure the email address shown matches your BudgetBox account
  3. Send the email — we will process your request within 30 days
  4. You will receive a confirmation email once your account has been deleted

Data kept after deletion

We do not retain any personal data after deletion. Anonymized, non-identifiable analytics (e.g., crash reports) may be retained for up to 90 days as required by our analytics provider.
