Privacy Policy

BudgetBox — Personal Finance & Budget Tracker

Last updated: April 25, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to BudgetBox ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and any related services (collectively, the "App").

Please read this policy carefully. If you disagree with its terms, please discontinue use of the App.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us and information generated by your use of the App.

Account Information

  • Email address (used for account creation and login)
  • Password (stored securely and never in plain text)

Financial Data

  • Income records (amount, source, date, category)
  • Expense records (amount, description, date, category)
  • Budget goals and spending limits you set
  • Receipt images you choose to scan (processed locally on your device)

Device Information

  • Device type and operating system version (for compatibility purposes)
  • Crash reports and performance diagnostics (anonymous)

We do not collect your bank account numbers, credit card numbers, or any payment credentials. BudgetBox is a manual-entry and receipt-scanning tracker — it does not connect to your bank.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To provide, operate, and maintain the App's core features
  • To sync your data across devices when you are signed in
  • To generate budget summaries, reports, and insights within the App
  • To respond to your support requests
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues
  • To display relevant advertisements (see Section 6)
  • To improve and personalize the App experience

4. Data Storage and Security

Your account data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database provider, with data encrypted at rest and in transit using TLS/SSL. Authentication tokens are stored securely on your device using platform-provided secure storage (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore).

The App also supports an offline / demo mode where your data is stored only on your device and never transmitted to our servers.

While we implement industry-standard security measures, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account and financial data for as long as your account is active. You may delete your account and all associated data at any time from the App's settings. Upon deletion, your data will be permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.

6. Advertising

BudgetBox uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain device identifiers and usage data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. Google's use of advertising data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking → disable for BudgetBox

7. Third-Party Services

The App integrates the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase — cloud database and authentication (Privacy Policy)
  • Google AdMob — advertising (Privacy Policy)
  • Expo / EAS — app build and update infrastructure (Privacy Policy)

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

8. Camera and Biometric Permissions

Camera: Used solely to scan receipts within the App. Images are processed on your device and are not uploaded to our servers unless you explicitly save a receipt entry.

Biometric (Face ID / Fingerprint): Used only for local authentication to unlock the App quickly. Biometric data never leaves your device and is handled entirely by the operating system's secure enclave.

9. Children's Privacy

BudgetBox is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately and we will delete it promptly.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Withdraw consent at any time (where processing is based on consent)
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address below.
